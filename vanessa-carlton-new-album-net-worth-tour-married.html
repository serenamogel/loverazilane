Vanessa Carlton Makes Comeback With New Album 'Love Is An Art'

July 18, 2024
blog

'A Thousand Miles' singer Vanessa Carlton's sixth studio album 'Love Is An Art' releases on 27 March 2020.

This new album is her comeback in the music industry after fading away from the spotlight for nearly five years. She had brought her fifth album entitled 'Liberman' back in 2015.

'Love Is An Art' consists of eleven songs including I Can't Stay the Same, Companion Star, I Know You Don't Mean It, 'Die, Dinosaur,' love is an Art, Future Plan, Back To Life, Patience, The Only Way To Love, Salesman, and Miner's Canary.

Net Worth Details; The Upcoming Tours Of Vanessa Are Canceled

Vanessa Carlton was set to tour to the cities in the United States and Canada from the mid-2020. However, her tours and concerts have been canceled due to the outbreak of pandemic Coronavirus (COVID-19).

As reported, Vanessa would have performed at Vancouver British Columbia, Canada, on 13 June 2020. Similarly, she would have toured to St. Louis, Minneapolis, Chicago, and Cleveland on 2 April, 4 April, 5/6 April, 13 April, respectively.

Vanessa is a famous American singer and songwriter who have already brought five studio albums, including Be Not Nobody (2002), Harmonium (2004), Heroes & Thieves (2007), Rabbits On The Sun (2011), and Liberman (2014). She is widely known for her hit songs like A Thousand Miles, White Houses, Nolita Fairytale, I Don't Want To Be A Bride, Blue Pool, Paradise, and many more.

With her consistent credits on the music industry, Vanessa Carlton has derived the net worth of $12 million.
