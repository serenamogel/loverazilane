Her Spouse Is A Talent Agent

JR Ringer was a talent agent at ICM, but he left the organization to join other firms. And, on February 2022, Verve confirmed that he had joined them as a talent agent.

According to Deadline, a few clients followed him to Verve, including John Hartnett, Sean Bean, Andy Allo, David Costabile, Isaiah Hill, Adam Nagaitis, Jessica Stroup, and Levi Miller.

He had worked at ICM for 14 years but thought that Veve would be a better place for him following ICM’s proposed acquisition by CAA. JR began his agency career there, starting in the mailroom shortly after graduating from Indiana University’s Kelly School of Business.

He worked in various positions after his experience in the mailroom. He worked under Jack Gilardi, Carter Cohn, and Chris Silbermann before being promoted to one of the first coordinators and agents at the reformed ICM Partners.

Merritt Patterson and Daniel Lissing in Catering Christmas

Merritt Patterson’s new movie, Catering Christmas, premiered on October 29, 2022. The new Christmas film from GAC Family revolves around a young caterer who falls for her boss’s nephew.

According to the movie’s synopsis, the perfectionist director of the renowned Harrison Foundation, Jean Harrison, hires a young caterer, Molly Frost, to look over this year’s annual Christmas Gala dinner.

However, things get complicated when Molly falls for Jean’s nephew, Carson, who is in his 30s. He is a travel photographer with no ambition to take over the family foundation. But that is until his aunt assigns him the duty of ensuring the catered dinner goes without any errors.

In the movie, Patterson romances Lissing. She plays Molly, the caterer, whereas Daniel portrays the role of Carson, the travel photographer in his 30s.
