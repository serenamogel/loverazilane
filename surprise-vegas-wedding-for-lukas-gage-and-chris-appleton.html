Surprise Vegas Wedding for Lukas Gage and Chris Appleton

September 02, 2024
blog

Hollywood actor Lukas Gage, known for his role in “White Lotus”, and celebrity hairstylist Chris Appleton have officially become spouses with a surprise wedding ceremony in Vegas.

Public records from Clark County Clerk’s office indicate that Lukas, 27, and Chris, 39, obtained their marriage license on Saturday, April 22. The private wedding ceremony took place at the Little White Chapel in Vegas.

A report from TMZ revealed that the wedding was a very intimate gathering, and among the attendees was none other than Kim Kardashian.

Lukas and Chris reportedly got engaged just weeks after announcing their relationship on “The Drew Barrymore Show”. Their romance became public in February during a vacation in Punta Mita, Mexico.
