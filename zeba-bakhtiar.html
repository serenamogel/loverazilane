Zeba Bakhtiar is a popular Pakistani film and television actress, and television director who has worked in the numbers of Bollywood and Urdu films. Zeba Bakhtiar film “Laal Kabootar” was selected in Oscar.  She was born on 5th November 1962 in Quetta, Baluchistan, Pakistan. Bakhtiar’s father was the former attorney general and a layer, Yahya Bakhtiar. Her father belongs to an ethnic Pashtun family from Quetta, whereas her mother is a Magyar or Hungarian. In 1993, Bakhtiar got married to a popular Pakistani singer Adnan Sami Khan. From which she has a son, Azaan. But the couple got divorced in 1997. She took custody of her son.

Career Journey

Zeba Bakhtiar began her screen career with the television debut in 1988, with the Pakistan Television Corporation drama serial “Anarkali” which was aired on PTV. The drama is the classical story of the Mughal emperor. After this drama, she has played very versatile roles in numerous Pakistani classical dramas such as her very famous horror drama is “Muqaddas” in 1996. This is the story of a hunted girl who lives with her parents near the forest.
