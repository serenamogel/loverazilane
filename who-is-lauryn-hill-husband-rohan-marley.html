Lauryn Noelle Hill, an American singer, songwriter, rapper, record producer, and actress was born on May 26, 1975.

In the hip-hop group Fugees, the 47-year-old first achieved chart success alongside Pras Michel and Wyclef Jean.

Then, in 1998, she went solo and issued Miseducation, which has now sold more than 20 million copies worldwide.

The album won five Grammy Awards in addition to shattering the US first-week sales record for a solo female artist.

The American soap series As The World Turns also featured Lauryn in a recurring role before she co-starred with Whoopi Goldberg in Sister Act 2 in 1993.

Who is Lauryn Hill husband Rohan Marley?

An entrepreneur and former football player from Jamaica, Rohan Anthony Marley was born on May 19, 1972.

He is the father of the football player Nico Marley and the model Selah Marley. He is the son of the reggae musician Bob Marley and Janet Hunt.

In 1996, Lauryn began dating businessman Rohan Marley. Before divorcing in 2009, the couple had five children: Zion David, Selah Louise, Joshua Omaru, John Nesta, and Sarah.
