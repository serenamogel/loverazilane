What is David Pollack's net worth?

Former Georgia defender David Pollack was an All-American in the early 2000s. At Georgia, he won the SEC Defensive Player of the Year award twice. The Cincinnati Bengals selected him in the first round of the 2005 NFL Draft. During the 2011 fall season, Pollack started working for ESPN's College GameDay.

In 2023, Pollack's estimated net worth is $986,300.

Pollack has two of the top five single-season sack records in program history (14 in 2002 and 12.5 in 2004). During his sophomore and senior years, Pollack ranked first in the SEC for sacks and tackles for loss.

Why did David Pollack retire early from the NFL?

During his time at Georgia, David Pollack recorded 36 sacks, which ranks third in NCAA history. He was selected in the first round of the 2005 NFL Draft and competed professionally for the Cincinnati Bengals in the NFL. When the Bengals played the Cleveland Browns in the second game of his second season, he sustained a neck injury that would end his career.

Marvin Lewis, the Bengals' then-head coach, later said that Pollack was "not completely comfortable with where he was medically" and would be retiring.
