Since 1992, Mark Steinberg has been representing elite golfers as a sports agent. He leads the professional golf section of Excel Sports Management, where he is a partner. Excel Sports Management, which was founded in 2002, is a full-service sports management and marketing firm that represents elite players in professional basketball, baseball, and golf. Tiger Woods stated on June 6, 2011, that he will be attending Excel with Steinberg. Since then, Steinberg has continued to expand his division, adding three-time PGA Tour champion Matt Kuchar and Danielle Kang to his roster. He formerly worked at IMG, a major corporate sports agency, where he managed the worldwide golf business.  Many players’ careers were influenced by Steinberg when he was there, notably Tiger Woods, Steve Stricker, and Annika Sörenstam. Tiger Woods has been represented by Steinberg since 1998. Steinberg has been dubbed “the de facto chief executive of (Tiger’s) golf and endorsement business” and one of the “three or four most influential individuals in golf” as his agent for Tiger Woods. Mr. Woods is “one of my closest pals,” according to Steinberg. IMG and Steinberg parted ways in May 2011. The Tiger Woods Foundation Board of Governors, the Annika Sörenstam Foundation Board, and the Cleveland Sports Commission all have Steinberg on their boards.
