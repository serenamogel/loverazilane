Jack in the Box Holiday Hours

October 03, 2024
blog

Popular fast-food chain, Jack in the Box, has over 2,200 locations across the United States, and it is especially popular in the western part of the country. While it is open for most of the year, the company does observe certain holidays and may have different hours of operation on those days. 

So, I decided to take a closer look at the Jack in the Box Holiday Hours, including which holidays the company observes and what the hours of operation are on those days.

An Overview of Jack in the Box

Jack in the Box was founded in 1951, and the company has a long history of providing quick and convenient meals to customers. Since its inception, it has been known for its burgers, chicken sandwiches, and tacos, as well as a variety of sides and drinks. The chain is also known for its drive-thru service, which allows customers to order and receive their food without leaving their cars.

In addition to its classic menu items, it also offers a variety of seasonal and limited-time offerings. For example, the chain has introduced menu items such as pumpkin spice lattes and turkey burgers during the fall and Thanksgiving, respectively.

They have a mascot too!

Jack in the Box branches are known for their fun and playful branding, which includes a mascot named Jack, who is a clown with a round head and a beanie. Customers can earn rewards by joining “Jack’s Pack,” which allows them to earn points for every purchase and redeem them for exclusive offers and discounts.

Regular Operating Hours

The restaurant is known for its late-night hours. Jack in the Box operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, at most of its locations. This means that customers can visit the restaurant at any time of the day or night to grab a quick meal or snack.

But are all locations open 24 hours a day?

Not all Jack in the Box locations are open 24 hours a day. A few have limited hours of operation, such as being open from 6:00 a.m. to midnight.

Jack in the Box Holiday Hours

1 New Year’s Day

On New Year’s Day, all Jack in the Box locations are open 24 hours. This means you can enjoy your favorite fast food items any time of day, whether you’re celebrating the start of the new year or just need a late-night snack.

2 Valentine’s Day

Jack in the Box is also open 24 hours on Valentine’s Day, so you can surprise your loved one with a special meal or treat yourself to something delicious.

3 Easter

Easter is a holiday that falls on a different date each year. Whenever the date, even on Easter Sunday, Jack in the Box is open 24 hours as usual.

4 Memorial Day

On Memorial Day, all locations are open 24 hours. This is a great opportunity to grab a quick bite to eat while you’re out enjoying the holiday.

5 Independence Day

On Independence Day (4th of July), Jack in the Box locations are typically open, but the hours of operation may vary. Some locations may be open for a limited time, while others may be open for their normal 24-hour business hours.

6 Labor Day

Like Memorial Day, Jack in the Box is open 24 hours on Labor Day. This is a perfect opportunity to grab a quick bite to eat while you’re out enjoying the holiday.

7 Thanksgiving

Jack in the Box is also open 24 hours on Thanksgiving, so you can celebrate America’s harvest feast with a delicious meal or snack any time of day.

8 Christmas Day

Jack in the Box is closed on Christmas Day, but you can still enjoy their menu on Christmas Eve.

It is important to note that the holiday hours may vary by location. Several restaurants may run “modified hours,” which often exclude midnight operation. As such, it is always a good idea to check with your local restaurant to confirm their hours on a specific holiday.

What Should You Do if You Need To Visit Jack in the Box on a Holiday?

If your local restaurant is closed, there are a few options you can consider:

  • Check to see if there are any other Jack in the Box locations in your area that are open on the holiday. As I mentioned, only a select few restaurants are closed on holidays. Visit another location, and you’ll most likely find it open.
  • Consider visiting a different fast food chain that is open on the holiday. Many will be open, so you may be able to find another restaurant that is open and serving food.
  • Consider ordering food from Jack in the Box for pickup or delivery. They offer pickup and delivery options through a variety of online platforms, including their own website and app. This can be a convenient way if the restaurant closest to your home is closed on a holiday.

A Few Tips That Can Help Make Your Jack in the Box Holiday Experience More Enjoyable

  • Plan ahead: Make sure to check the hours of operation for your local Jack in the Box before heading out on a holiday. This will help you avoid any disappointment if the restaurant is closed or has modified hours.
  • Call ahead: If you are unsure of the holiday hours, it can be helpful to call ahead and confirm. This will ensure that you don’t make a trip out to the restaurant only to find that it is closed.
  • Consider ordering ahead: If you know you will be visiting Jack in the Box on a holiday, you may want to consider placing an order ahead of time. This can help save time and ensure that you get your food as soon as you arrive at the restaurant.
  • Be patient: Holidays can be busy times for fast food restaurants, so be prepared for longer wait times and crowds. It can be helpful to bring something to do while you wait, such as a book or a phone to play games on.
