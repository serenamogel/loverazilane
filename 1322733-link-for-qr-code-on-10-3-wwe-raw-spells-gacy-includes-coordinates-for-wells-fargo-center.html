Link For QR Code On 10/3 WWE RAW Spells Gacy, Includes Coordinates For Wells Fargo Center

June 30, 2024
blog

The White Rabbit mystery continues.

In recent weeks, WWE has been showing QR codes during episodes of and SmackDown, and they have offered a number of hints about the White Rabbit.

Another code appeared during the October 3 episode of RAW.

The newest WR code and latest set of White Rabbit clues. ? #WWERaw #WhiteRabbit #BrayWyatt pic.twitter.com/rQ8uC8ff2b

— TheRingsideRoster.com (@RingsideRoster) October 4, 2022

The code when to a page that showed two different paintings, depending on whether it was opened in a mobile browser or on a desktop. Per Ryan Satin, there is a Magic Eye that reads “MTA4Mjl,” which is the SKU code for White Rabbit milk. Satin also noted that if you put the code into a Base64 decoder, it translates to: “10822”, which is seemingly October 9, 2022, the date of WWE Extreme Rules.

This is what comes up when you google the code in the Samson photo: pic.twitter.com/942QdqPnmn

Likewise, the numbers on the page, 7517202, are part of the coordinates for the Wells Fargo Center, the host venue of Extreme Rules.

The binary code in the link translates to “GACY”, seemingly a nod to NXT star Joe Gacy.

pic.twitter.com/R07qHyUSnK

— Robert DeFelice | Fightful, WrestleZone (@dudefelice) October 4, 2022

One painting tells the biblical story of Samson and Delilah. Samson was anointed by God and renowned for his superhuman strength. Per Satin, “Chosen by God, Samson was blessed with superhuman strength that made him invincible as long as the hair on his head remained unshorn. When he told this secret to his Philistine wife Delilah, he was captured and blinded and his hair was cut. He regained his strength as soon as his hair grew back and he was able to destroy the Philistine temple, killing 3,000 Philistines.”

Yet another clue was in the Predator language contained on the QR code page, which translates to, “Before me things created were none, save things. Eternal, and eternal I endure.”
