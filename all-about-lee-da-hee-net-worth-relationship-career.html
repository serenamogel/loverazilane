Lee Da-hee is a South Korean model and actress who rose to international recognition as a result of her performances in K-dramas such as the courtroom drama I Can Hear Your Voice. She has worked as a professional actor for about two decades.

What is the Net Worth of Lee Da Hee? Salary, Earnings

The actress’s net worth is expected to reach around $1 million by March 2021. She made the most of her money by working in the entertainment sector.

Da Hee has appeared as an actress in a number of television episodes, films, and other ventures. She has also hosted the Golden Disc Awards thrice. She began her modeling career in 2002, reaching the finals of the SBS Super Elite Model Contest.

As a result of her modeling career, she began to be cast in various TV shows. In 2003, she had a minor role in Thousand Years of Love. In 2013, she made her debut as Seo Do-Yeon in the film “I Can Hear Your Voice.”

Where was Lee Da Hee born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Lee Da-hee was born on March 15, 1985, and will be 36 in March 2021. Her birthplace is the Republic of Korea. She went to Yongin High School and then Sejong University. She developed an early interest in acting and began modeling and acting classes as a child.

Keeping with the theme, she was a finalist in the 2002 SBS Super Elite Model Contest. Following her first breakthrough, she began to land supporting and lead roles in a number of well-known television shows.
